Our Story

From a small print shop in 1994 to one of Ireland's most trusted custom printing companies. Three decades of putting your designs on products people love to wear.

Est. 1994

30+ Years of Printing Excellence

Dublin T-Shirt Print started in 1994 with a simple mission: provide high-quality custom printed garments at fair prices, with a turnaround time that actually works for our customers.

Three decades later, that mission hasn't changed. We've grown from t-shirts to a full range of custom printed products — hoodies, hats, mugs, bags, workwear, and sports jerseys — but our commitment to quality, speed, and value remains the same.

As an Irish-owned and operated business, we understand what our customers need. Whether it's a rush order for a charity event, a season's worth of jerseys for the local GAA club, or branded workwear for a growing business, we treat every order like it matters — because it does.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth

1994

The Beginning

Founded as a small screen printing operation, serving local businesses and sports clubs in the midlands.

2000s

Expanding Our Range

Added vinyl printing and full-colour transfer capabilities. Began serving customers nationwide with our Dublin delivery network.

2010s

Going Digital

Invested in sublimation technology for mugs and sportswear. Launched online ordering and expanded our product range to include workwear and accessories.

2020s

DTF & Beyond

Adopted DTF (Direct-to-Film) printing technology for unlimited colour, no-minimum orders. Now serving thousands of customers across Ireland with six printing methods.

Our Values

What Sets Us Apart

Quality First

Every item is quality-checked before it leaves our workshop. We use premium inks, garments, and techniques to ensure your prints look great and last.

Speed That Delivers

Our 3-4 day turnaround is one of the fastest in Ireland. When you need it fast, we deliver — without cutting corners on quality.

Fair Pricing

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. Free artwork, free design consultation, and big savings on bulk orders. We believe quality printing shouldn't cost a fortune.

Personal Service

We're real people, not a faceless corporation. Call us, WhatsApp us, email us — you'll always talk to someone who knows your order.

Irish-Owned

Proudly Irish-owned and operated since 1994. Your money supports a local business and keeps printing jobs in Ireland.

Eco-Conscious

We use water-based inks wherever possible, offer organic cotton options, and minimise waste throughout our production process.

Let's Work Together

30+ years of experience at your service. Tell us what you need and we'll make it happen.
