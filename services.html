How We Print

Six professional printing methods, each matched to your product for the best quality and value. We'll recommend the right technique for your project — just tell us what you need.

Our Process

How It Works

From your idea to the finished product in four simple steps.

Tell Us What You Need

Fill out our quick quote form or give us a call. Tell us the product, quantity, and any design ideas. No need for final artwork at this stage.

Free Artwork & Design

Our design team creates or refines your artwork at no extra cost. We'll send a digital proof for your approval before anything goes to print.

We Print Your Order

Once you approve the proof, we get to work. We match the optimal printing method to your product and run a quality check on every item.

Delivered to Your Door

Your order is packaged and shipped within 3-4 working days. Flat rate €12.50 per box anywhere in Ireland. Collection also available.

Printing Methods

Six Ways to Print

Each technique has its strengths. We'll help you choose the right one.

🎨

Screen Printing

The industry gold standard. Water-based inks are pushed through a fine mesh screen to create vibrant, long-lasting prints. The more you order, the cheaper it gets — making screen printing ideal for bulk orders of 25 or more. Best for: bold logos, simple designs, large quantities.

Best for: T-shirts, hoodies, tote bags
Min. order: 25 units
Colours: Up to 6 spot colours
🌈

DTF (Direct-to-Film) Printing

The newest technology in garment printing. A full-colour design is printed onto a special film and heat-pressed onto your garment. No colour limits, no minimums, photographic quality. DTF works on virtually any fabric colour including dark garments.

Best for: Complex designs, photos, small runs
Min. order: 1 unit
Colours: Unlimited full colour
✏️

Vinyl Printing

Cut vinyl is heat-pressed onto garments for a bold, raised finish. Ideal for names, numbers, and simple text-based designs. Extremely durable and available in a wide range of colours including metallic, glitter, and reflective finishes.

Best for: Names & numbers, sports jerseys
Min. order: 1 unit
Colours: Single colour per layer
📸

Full-Colour Transfer

Full-colour designs are printed onto transfer paper and heat-pressed onto garments. Great for photographic images, detailed illustrations, and designs with many colours. Works well for small to medium runs where screen printing isn't cost-effective.

Best for: Photos, detailed artwork, small runs
Min. order: 1 unit
Colours: Unlimited full colour
💧

Sublimation Printing

Heat converts solid dye into gas which bonds with polyester fibres. The result is a print that becomes part of the fabric — it won't crack, peel, or fade. Sublimation allows edge-to-edge all-over printing and is the go-to for sportswear and mugs.

Best for: Sportswear, mugs, all-over prints
Min. order: 1 unit
Colours: Unlimited full colour
🏷️

Label Printing

Add a professional touch to your custom garments with branded labels. We produce woven labels, printed satin labels, and heat transfer labels for neck, hem, and care instructions. Perfect for turning custom prints into your own clothing line.

Best for: Brand labels, clothing lines
Min. order: 100 units
Types: Woven, satin, heat transfer
